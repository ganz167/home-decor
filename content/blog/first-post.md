Small spaces need smart styling. Here are 5 tips:

**1. Use light colors**  
White and beige make rooms feel bigger.

**2. Add mirrors**  
Mirrors bounce light and create depth.

**3. Multi-function furniture**  
Ottomans with storage = less clutter.
